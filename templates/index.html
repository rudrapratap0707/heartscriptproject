About HeartScript

Welcome to HeartScript, a sanctuary where emotions find their form through the timeless art of handwriting and personalized creativity. In a world that is increasingly becoming digital and automated, we believe that the soul of a message lies in the effort, the curves of the ink, and the personal touch that only a human hand can provide. HeartScript was born out of a simple yet profound realization: the most meaningful gifts aren't just objects; they are stories, memories, and deep-seated feelings captured on paper. Our journey started with a passion for ink and a desire to bring back the intimacy of personal touch in gifting.

Our platform is dedicated to those who cherish the "old-school" charm of handwritten letters, customized journals, and artistic scripts. We specialize in transforming your raw emotions into beautiful masterpieces that can be touched, felt, and preserved for a lifetime. Whether it is a confession of love, a letter of gratitude to a parent, or a personalized piece of art for a friend, HeartScript ensures that every word resonates with the heartbeat of the sender. We don't just sell products; we facilitate a connection between hearts that transcends the boundaries of screens and keyboards. Every stroke of our pen is deliberate, designed to evoke the exact emotion you intend to share.

At HeartScript, we take immense pride in our craftsmanship. Every order placed on our website is handled with extreme delicacy and attention to detail. Our team of artists and calligraphers understands that when you trust us with your message, you are trusting us with a piece of your heart. That is why we use premium quality materials—from rich, textured papers to specialized archival inks—that reflect the premium nature of your sentiments. Our process is deeply collaborative; we listen to your stories and customize every script to match the mood and occasion perfectly, ensuring that the final result is nothing short of magical.

As we look towards the future, HeartScript remains committed to reviving the lost art of personal correspondence. We envision a world where people take a moment to slow down and express themselves more deeply, moving away from the ephemeral nature of instant messaging. Our website is more than an e-commerce store; it is a movement to bring back the warmth of human connection. By choosing HeartScript, you are not just buying a gift; you are celebrating the beauty of being human, the power of words, and the magic of a script that comes straight from the heart. Join us in our journey of scripting love, one letter at a time, and let us help you create a legacy that lasts forever.

Crafted with Love, Scripted for You.

Service Guidelines

Shipping Policy

At HeartScript, every piece is handcrafted with love. Once an order is placed, it takes 2-4 business days for our artisans to complete the calligraphy and preparation. After dispatch, standard shipping takes 5-7 business days across India. We provide real-time tracking details via WhatsApp as soon as your package leaves our studio. For urgent gifting, please contact our support team.

Replacement Policy

Since our products are personalized masterpieces, we do not offer returns. However, we provide a 7-Day Replacement Guarantee if the product arrives damaged or contains a clerical error from our side. To claim, an unboxing video is mandatory. Please share the video within 24 hours of delivery at +91 6394174932 to initiate a swift replacement.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is as sacred as your emotions. HeartScript collects minimal data—name, contact, and address—strictly for order fulfillment. We use Cloudinary for secure profile image storage and your security questions are encrypted. We never share your personal letters or custom details with third parties. Your stories are safe in our vault, scripted only for the eyes they are meant for.
